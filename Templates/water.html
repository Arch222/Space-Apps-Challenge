Water quality essentially measures the health of the surface water in terms of nutrients and algae growth. Water quality was measured using a time-series app that tracked medians of cholrophyll concentration over a period of two years for all available locations.Cholophyll is a reasonable measure of water quality as it tracks the amount of algae present in a specific body of water.
